About Sports Facilities

Your heart is a muscle and needs frequent exercise to help it keep fit and healthy.When you are physically active your mind gets a chance to unplug from daily stresses and strains of life.NIT Agartala has the biggest Sports complex across all the government universities in india include both NIT’s and IIT’s. NIT Agartala has a lot to offer in sports. If you are good at something, you will surely be encouraged. Provisions are made available for almost every sport which is commonly played like cricket, football, tt, badminton, athletics, basketball, kabaddi, kho-kho, volleyball, handball, gym, indoors etc. An intramural Sports meet allows them to show their mettle and broaden their horizons. The institute believes that playing sports helps the students to build a spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship which will help them to lead better lives.
