Sponsored research project

The Civil Engineering department at NIT Agartala is conducting diverse research projects funded by various Indian government agencies:

• Innovation & Technology: i-TBI (DST) supports technological innovation (₹419.9 Lakhs, 2022-2026).

• Disaster Mitigation & Materials: FIST Project (DST) focuses on sustainable materials (₹88 Lakhs).

• Earthquake-Resistant Housing: SERB funds seismic block research (₹40 Lakhs).

• Biogas Purification: CSIR project converts CO₂ to CH₄ (₹23.93 Lakhs).

Environmental & Regional Studies:

• Arsenic Contamination in Groundwater (STIC-ISRO, ₹21 Lakhs).

• Weather Impact on Tea Cultivation (STIC-ISRO, ₹19.8 Lakhs).

• Seismic Vulnerability of RC Buildings (CSIR, ₹20.21 Lakhs).

Advanced Engineering Solutions:

• Low-Cost Seismic Vibration Control (SERB, ₹25.58 Lakhs).

• Self-Centering Concrete Structures (Virginia Tech, ₹13 Lakhs).

• Antimicrobial Resistance: DST funds Fluoroquinolone Antibiotics study (₹100 Lakhs).
