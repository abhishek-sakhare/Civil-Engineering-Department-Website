Inter-Department Football Champion - Civil Engg. Dept. Team

Under the banner of Swachhata Hi Seva, a series of sports activities were organized at the ABV Sports Complex, NIT Agartala. This initiative aims to promote fitness, teamwork, and the values of cleanliness and community service. Our enthusiastic participants embraced the spirit of competition while reinforcing the message of a cleaner, greener India.

In a thrilling culmination of the event, our Civil Engineering Team whose captain was Maxon Debbarma emerged as the champions, earning well-deserved accolades for their stellar performance. Their journey to victory was marked by unity, determination, and an unwavering spirit.

Thank you to everyone involved for making this event a success!
