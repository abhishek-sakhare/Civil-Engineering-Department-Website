Expert Lecture on Wastewater Treatment & ZLD

The Civil Engineering Department of NIT Agartala organized an expert lecture by Prof. Bhim Charan Meikap, Professor at IIT Kharagpur, during his visit from 20th to 22nd November 2025 in connection with the PhD viva-voce of Mr. Akash Deb (17DECER001).

Prof. Meikap delivered a talk on “Wastewater Treatment and Surface Run-Off Water Management towards ZLD in Fertilizer Plant” on 21st November 2025 at the Conference Room of the department. The session highlighted sustainable wastewater practices, industrial run-off management, and approaches to achieving Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD).

The program received active participation from PhD scholars, M.Tech students, and faculty members, making it an informative and enriching academic experience for all.

PhD Defense Seminar Conducted in Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Engineering Department of NIT Agartala successfully conducted the PhD defense viva-voce seminar of Mr. Akash Deb (Enrollment No: 17DECER001), Research Scholar of the department, on 21st November 2025.

He presented his PhD thesis titled “Fabrication and Characterization of Polyaniline/Metal Oxide Based Novel Nanocomposites for Toxic Dye Loaded Wastewater Remediation” in the Conference Room of the department. The seminar discussed innovative nanocomposite-based solutions for treating dye-contaminated wastewater, contributing to ongoing research in sustainable water treatment technologies.

The session was attended by members of the Oral Examination Board, faculty members, PhD scholars, and students, making it an engaging and informative academic event.

Symposium on "Soft measures for flood and erosion control" on 26th July, 2022

A Symposium on Soft measures for flood and erosion control was organized by Civil Engineering Department NIT Agartala in association with Bramhaputra Board, Agartala Division under Ministry of Jal Shakti, Govt. of India on 26th July,2022. The event was organized in contemplation of celebrating 75th anniversary of India's Independence Azadi ka Amrit Mohatsav. The inauguration of the session was started by lightening a lamp by the dignitaries on the stage and a welcome address by HOD Civil Engineering Department NIT Agartala. The session was chaired by Chief Guest-Prof. H.K. Sharma, Director NIT Agartala; Chief Engineer (WR), Govt. of Tripura; HOD(Civil Engineering Department); Deputy Chief Engineer Brahmaputra Board; Government of India and other profound dignitaries. The symposium was attended by the research scholars, students and practicing engineers from all over the state of Tripura. The expert speakers and other dignitaries stressed on the importance of research in the field of soft measures for flood and erosion control in the basin of river Bramhaputra and other northeastern flood prone areas. Finally, Er. S.K. Deb, Executive Engineer. Brahmaputra Board, briefed about the activities and roles of Brahmaputra Board and delivered vote of thanks. They also assured that such events would be organized in bigger forums in future to raise awareness for all stakeholders to frame policy decisions.
