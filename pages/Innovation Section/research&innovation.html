The Innovative event "Arjuna 1.0 Hackathon" organized by Civil Innovation and Technology Cell, NIT Agartala

Arjuna 1.0 was part of the North East Tech Summit 2024, a prestigious hackathon event where teams competed to showcase their innovative technological solutions. The event focused on solving real-world problems through technology and innovation.The hackathon highlighted the importance of collaboration, creativity, and technical skills, making it a platform for budding developers and innovators to shine. Winning this competition is a testament to team's dedication and technical excellence.

Arjuna 1.0 featured total 3 problem statements, namely as 1. Mental Well Being Surveillance, Tracking and Assessment; 2.Advancing Landslide Detection and Early Warning System; 3.Groundwater Recharge. Every problem statements had 5 grand finalists team among them 1 team had won the grand finale and awarded with 20,000 RS. Many teams from different colleges of india had participated in this hackathon among them one team from NIT Agartala had the Hackathon in Mental Well Being Surveillance, Tracking and Assessment problem statement.
