Research Scholar Fest

The eagerly awaited Research Scholar Fest is here, providing a platform to showcase the academic brilliance and innovative research of scholars and students across all levels at NIT Agartala. This fest is designed to inspire, encourage, and recognize the efforts of our budding researchers.

Poster Presentation by PhD Scholars: All PhD scholars, irrespective of their semester, are invited to participate and present their research through engaging and insightful posters.

Poster Presentation by PG Students: Postgraduate students in their 2nd year will have the opportunity to exhibit their academic projects and research findings.

UG Students Participation: Undergraduate students from the 2nd year onwards are encouraged to participate in the poster or oral presentations, making this a truly inclusive event.
